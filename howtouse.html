How to Use Hazardify.AI

Welcome to Hazardify.AI! This guide will walk you through the steps to effectively use our tool.

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Navigate to our main page and click on the "Choose file" button. Select the image you wish to analyze from your device.

Step 2: Image Preview

Upon selection, you can view the image by clicking on the preview image button. You can review the image to ensure it's the correct one for analysis.

Step 3: Analysis

Our AI will process the image and provide you with insights. The results will include classifications or predictions based on the image content.

Step 4: Interpret Results

Review the provided information to gain insights into the image. The tool offers a guide to understanding the potential categories or features detected in your image.

Step 5: Safety First

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. No private data will be accessed, stored, or retained in our database at any time during your use of Hazardify.ai. Rest assured, your files and data remain completely secure and private when utilizing our tool.

Please note: While we strive for accuracy, Hazardify.AI may not always provide correct results. Use it as a guide rather than a definitive answer.
